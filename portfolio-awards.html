  • Champion

    Integrated Southern Tagalog Association of Information Technology Association's iSIte 2018 C Programming Competition

    Held at the Batangas State, University - Malvar Campus. Competing against 30+ Universities on the whole Region. To recognize the potentials of students to excel in the field of Information Technology and its applications through solving machine problems in a limited time using a specific Programming Language. The contestant that solves the most problems in the fewest attempts in the least cumulative time will be declared the winner.
